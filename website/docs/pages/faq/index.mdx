## How the documentation is organized?

ArcBlock Blockchain has a lot of documentation. A high-level overview of how it’s organized will help you know where to look for certain things:

- [Getting Started](./quick-start) take you by the hand through a series of steps to sending your first transaction. Start here if you’re new to ArcBlock Blockchain.
- [Conceptual guides](./conceptual) discuss key concepts at a fairly high level and provide useful background information and explanation.
- [Reference guides](./reference) contain technical reference various aspects of ArcBlock Blockchain machinery. They describe how it works and how to use it but assume that you have a basic understanding of key concepts.
- [How-to guides](./how-to) are recipes. They guide you through the steps involved in addressing key problems and use-cases. They are more advanced than Getting Started and assume some knowledge of how ArcBlock Blockchain works.
